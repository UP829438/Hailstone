Welcome

This is my video page. The video was created as part of some coursework that I had to complete in 2015. The brief was to create a music video for a song of our choice. We could choose from three different styles of video. We could either make a narrative video, or an interpretive video. I chose to create a narrative video in the form of a stop motion. I tried to choose a shorter video as I knew that stop motion takes a very long time to produce.
